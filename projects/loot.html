A new Look, a new Loot

Using minimalist design and various materials, we crafted a unique shop in the heart of Dublin. The customers allowed us to use recycled materials, expanding our ideas to make the shopping experience more eco-friendly, as this is one of the most important parts of the concept. The results are just stunning – we created and crafted every small detail in the shop!

LOOT . Interior view from the clients perspectives.

Retail Shop and Sustainability

A very special project! We made everything’s from the first designs and ideias to the final product . Was full renovation in the building 4 floors , shop front , lighting , fit out , painting and much more .

Loot was a great opportunity for us to use different materials and mix some new ideas. We used in this project recycling sheets. something that we’re looking forward to promoting.
